Calculate BFS and move the best direction possible. It is working in different direction (finding path from end to start)

That's because it is way more efficient, because my next step is the last step calculated before finding finish.

Parameters
startStarting position (allways position of AI player)
endEnd position (allways position of other player)
m_pureMapDataMap data used for validation of steps
m_bombsIf player's positions are equal, new bomb is planted and unique pointer is added to MapContainer's m_bombs
